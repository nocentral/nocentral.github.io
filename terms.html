1. What NoCentral is

NoCentral is a read-mostly administrative client for HPE Aruba Networking Instant (“Aruba Instant”) Virtual Controller clusters. It connects to a Virtual Controller you own or are authorized to manage, reads operational data the controller has already collected, and presents it on iPad and iPhone.

The App is intended for Wi-Fi administrators, network professionals, and educational use by individuals working with their own Aruba Instant deployments.

All data the App displays originates from your own Aruba Virtual Controller’s existing observation and reporting capabilities, accessed via Aruba’s documented REST API.
