1. What NoCentral Is

NoCentral is a read-only administrative client for HPE Aruba Networking Instant Access Point (Aruba IAP) Virtual Controller clusters running AOS 8 (versions 8.10–8.13). It connects to a Virtual Controller you own or are authorised to manage, reads operational data the controller has already collected, and presents it on iPhone, iPad, and Apple Silicon Mac (via "Designed for iPad").

The App is intended for Wi-Fi administrators, network professionals, and educational use by individuals working with their own Aruba IAP deployments.

3. What NoCentral Does Not Do

For clarity:

  • NoCentral does not perform independent Wi-Fi scanning, packet capture, monitor-mode reception, or deep packet inspection
  • NoCentral does not transmit deauthentication frames, association attempts, probe responses, or any active reconnaissance against networks it is not authorised to administer

  • NoCentral does not automatically share, upload, or publish any observation data

All data the App displays originates from your own Aruba Virtual Controller's existing observation capabilities, accessed via Aruba's documented REST API. See the Privacy Policy for full detail on what data the App handles and where it goes.
